# Luminous-Glass — Moodboard (12 Calibration Anchors)

Twelve abstract exemplars of the genre, described precisely as compositions. Use them to calibrate: *would my output sit in the same family?* These describe RULES, not any real screen — treat them as archetypes to aim at, never as layouts to copy.

1. **Neon-green fintech noir** — Black hand-silhouette holds a phone: a "Savings" label white on noir; a GREEN accent card (+38%, bar chart) above white/gray/black cards in one bento; circular ring-progress arrow button; layered green area chart with age-cohort dots. *Anchors: Laws 1/2/7; bento surface-mixing; ring-CTA.*

2. **Ticker macro squiggle** — Cinematic macro of a noir screen: a giant money value, tiny green delta, relaxed YELLOW squiggle line ending in a dot with a dark pill tooltip (value · date); red-nailed hand. *Anchors: Law 4/9; squiggle+tooltip anatomy; noir+one-accent.*

3. **Retail lime split-screen** — Phone floating between black rocks: a sage-light top half (two-tone greeting "Hi there, *here's* **what's happening in your stores.**", month chips, bold six-figure money value + conversational caption) and a dark-olive bottom half (LIME progress bar on hatched track, olive cards with lime values). *Anchors: Laws 1/5/11; split-mode; hatched track; two-tone headline.*

4. **Lime/black engraved pair** — A white "Sales Analyzing" screen: an acid-lime Gross Profit card and a black Net Profit card stacked, both with debossed concentric-ring texture, a circular arrow button bridging them half-and-half; phone staged on perforated metal. *Anchors: Law 7; engrave texture; bridging button; industrial props.*

5. **Glowing-orb expenses** — Navy-noir "Expenses": a pie rendered as blurred glowing blue/violet/green orbs with thin white leader lines; a five-figure money value with a green currency mark; dark category cards with colored dots; glass bottom bar; staged on rock against a dusk sky. *Anchors: Laws 3/9; glow-as-data; category dot-coding.*

6. **Dot-matrix AI assistant** — A giant lowercase wordmark behind a phone on sage; an AI assistant screen: a red-pink halftone wave sphere, a LIME action pill with a black wand-circle, a white summary card, age-cohort chips. *Anchors: Laws 2/8/11 + AI-surface language; type-behind-device.*

7. **Acid inventory color-field** — An ORANGE poster field; a dark-olive dashboard with a YELLOW orders card (with a halftone patch), an orange stock donut, a yellow-highlighted map with a "Delay +2d" pill, a glass reorder panel with a product photo; mobile variant full-glass over black rock. *Anchors: Laws 1/2/7/9; acid triad; texture-coding; staging mode 2.*

8. **Datacenter LED heatmap** — Forest-green noir trio: a sensor grid of tinted chips (42°…88°) each with a colored LED underline (green→yellow→red), a symmetric multi-strand flow chart, LED bar-forest charts with a giant score value and a red delta. *Anchors: Laws 8/9/17; heatmap chips; bar-forest; status semantics.*

11. **Retreat booking** — A sunset-marsh photograph full-bleed (yellow chair accent); ONE tall glass card: photo header with chips, Booking/Payment sections split by hairlines, glass pills (More Details ⌄ / Deposit – €500 ⌄), a white Book pill below. *Anchors: Laws 6/12; photo-immersive minimalism; luxury calm.*

## Squint-test protocol

1. Put the candidate design next to anchors 3, 9, 10 (or the 3 closest by mode/domain).
2. Ask: same neutral poles? one accent, domain-correct? hero number present? glass recipe exact? atmosphere alive? charts house-style?
3. Any anti-pattern visible (SKILL.md list) → fail → fix the system value that drifted, not just the pixel.
